Discovery Session

Your first real conversation. No agenda, no performance — just an honest look at where you are and whether this kind of work fits.

+

"Most people arrive not knowing what to say. That's usually where the most important thing lives — just beneath the surface, waiting for someone to ask the right question."

The Discovery Session is a single, 60-minute video call. It's a space to lay down whatever you've been carrying — whether that's emotional pain, spiritual confusion, a life that no longer fits, or just the persistent feeling that something isn't right.

Hubert listens with the kind of depth that comes from twenty years of doing this work on himself — and what usually happens is that you leave understanding something you didn't understand when you arrived.

This session is also a natural starting point before committing to anything longer. Come once, see how it lands.

4 Sessions · 60 Min Each · 4–6 Weeks Most Common

The Journey

Four conversations over four to six weeks. Enough time to actually go somewhere — to understand a pattern, feel a shift, and arrive somewhere meaningfully different.

+

"One conversation can open a door. Four gives you enough time to actually walk through it."

The Journey is four 60-minute video sessions spread across four to six weeks. It's the format most people find most useful because it gives the work time to breathe — time for something to surface in session one, settle through the week, and deepen in session two.

There's no fixed curriculum. Each session responds to where you are right then — what's shifted, what's still pressing, what's become clearer or more confusing. Hubert holds a thread across the four conversations without forcing a direction.

What tends to happen over four sessions is that the surface story gives way to something underneath. The presenting problem turns out to point at something older, something that's been running quietly in the background for years. That's where the real movement happens.

You set the pace. Sessions can be weekly or fortnightly. Flexible to life.

Format

Four 60-minute Zoom calls over 4–6 weeks

Contribution

Donation after each session — or a total contribution at the end

This session fits if you are

  • Going through something you can feel but can't fully articulate
  • Noticing a recurring pattern in your relationships, reactions, or circumstances
  • In the middle of a spiritual awakening and need grounded support
  • Coming out of depression, grief, or a major life disruption
  • Ready to do real inner work, not just vent

What to expect

Book your first session to begin. Subsequent sessions are scheduled at the end of each call, so you're always in control of timing. Nothing is locked in beyond your willingness to show up.

Ongoing · Your Pace · No Fixed End

Open Continuity

For those navigating a longer passage — an awakening, a breakdown, a rebuilding. No fixed end. You come when you need it, as often as it helps.

+

"Some people need a single session. Others are in the middle of something that doesn't have a neat timeline. That's what this is for."

Open Continuity is an ongoing, open-ended working relationship with Hubert. There's no set number of sessions, no programme to complete, no certificate at the end. You book when you need a session, take breaks when life demands it, and return when something stirs.

This format is particularly suited to people going through a prolonged spiritual awakening — those disorienting, stretching periods where your sense of self is genuinely in question and the old frameworks have stopped working. It's also the right fit for anyone rebuilding after a significant collapse: a relationship, a career, an identity.

Long-term work creates a different quality of conversation. Hubert knows your landscape. Patterns become visible over months that couldn't be seen in weeks. The relationship itself becomes a kind of ground — something stable to return to when things inside are moving fast.

Format

60-minute Zoom sessions, booked as needed

Contribution

Donation after each session — completely at your discretion

This session fits if you are

  • Going through a prolonged or deep spiritual awakening
  • Rebuilding after a major life collapse
  • Someone who benefits from a consistent, trusted space over time
  • Navigating something that doesn't fit a neat four-week arc
  • Past the acute crisis but still in the long, quieter work of integration

What to expect

Start with a Discovery Session if you haven't spoken with Hubert before. Open Continuity begins from the second session onward — no contract, no minimum commitment. Just show up when it's useful.

Before You Book

Questions people usually have.

Is this therapy or counselling?

+

No. Still Ground is not a therapeutic service and Hubert is not a licensed therapist or clinical counsellor. What this is — honestly — is deeper than most therapy and different from all coaching. It's real conversation with someone who has navigated the same terrain from the inside. If you're in acute clinical crisis, please also seek professional clinical support. These sessions work best alongside life, not instead of it.

How does the donation model actually work?

+

After each session, you'll receive a link to contribute whatever felt true to the value you received. There's no minimum, no maximum, and no invoice. Some people give a little, some give a lot — and both are received with the same openness. The model exists because transformation doesn't come with a standardised price tag. You'll know what your session was worth. I trust you to honour that.

What platform do we use for video calls?

+

Zoom. You'll receive a link in your booking confirmation. No account required on your end — just click the link at the scheduled time. If you have strong feelings about a different platform, reach out and we can work something out.

Do I need to prepare anything before the session?

+

Nothing. You don't need notes, a list of topics, or a clear idea of what you want to say. Come as you are. The most honest starting point is usually "I don't know where to begin" — and that's more than enough to work with.

What if I'm not sure which session type is right for me?

+

Start with a Discovery Session. It exists precisely for that uncertainty. One conversation gives you everything you need to know whether you want to continue and in what form. Nothing is locked in from a single session.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

+

Yes — just give at least 24 hours' notice through your booking confirmation. Life happens. What matters is showing up when you're genuinely ready, not just when you originally planned to.
